THREE DASHBOARDS · ONE KIT

Tuning. Media. Nitrous.

Swap between three purpose-built screens with a tap. Every one reads live from your Corvette.

⛽ Swappable live gauge clusters

Big analog dials with proper redline arcs — MAF, boost, RPM, speed, ECT, AFR — plus 24 status cells for fuel trims, injector duty, knock retard, timing advance, and oil pressure. Swap layouts on the fly; multiple themes built in. Design your own cluster in the Gauge Builder and it ships pixel-identical to your dash.

  • Analog dials + 24-cell tuning readout
  • Trim-aware: boost gauge appears on Z06/ZR1
  • Passive OEM CAN read — no reflash

📺 250-app multimedia head unit

Ten tabs, twenty-five apps each: streaming music, video, live TV, news, sports, weather + maps, podcasts, social, productivity, and browser games. Netflix / Prime / Disney+ DRM works — Widevine is pre-installed. Bluetooth audio routes straight to your OEM stereo, no aux cable.

  • 250 launch tiles across 10 tabs
  • Widevine DRM pre-installed
  • Always-available draggable BACK + keyboard overlay

⚡ Multi-interlock nitrous control

A software-managed RPM-window switch with seven independent interlocks: physical arm switch, software arm, gear ≠ P/N, RPM in window, TPS at WOT, coolant warmed, bottle temp safe. Programmable purge bursts and a bottle-heater thermostat with hard cutoff. Every relay is fail-safe default-OFF.

  • 7-gate interlock stack
  • Programmable purge + bottle heater
  • Fail-safe default-OFF on every relay
SUPPORTED TRIMS

Trim-specific signal maps for all four variants. The Z06 / ZR1 boost gauge appears automatically when supercharger hardware is detected. Auto trans, M7 manual, and MagneRide are all auto-detected.
