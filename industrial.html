WHAT IT DOES

From one machine to a hundred and fifty.

🔌

Speaks the protocols

OPC UA, MQTT, and Modbus TCP/RTU out of the box. Talks to PLCs, HMIs, and controllers from the vendors already on your floor — WAGO, AutomationDirect, Siemens, Allen-Bradley, and more.

📊

Live operator dashboards

Clean, glanceable screens that show machine state, throughput, and alarms in real time — built for the floor, not the boardroom. Demo and live modes so you can train before you deploy.

💵

Per-shift cost analytics

Material and cost reporting per shift, day, month, and year — with editable BOMs, snapshots, and CSV export for accounting and purchasing. The numbers your finance team actually asks for.

Scales without rewriting

The same pattern that runs one blender runs a hundred and fifty. Time-series storage, dashboards, and alerting designed to grow with the plant, not get thrown away at the next tier.
