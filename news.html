C7 head unit hits hardware prototype

Three integrated dashboards — live tuning gauges, a 250-app multimedia center, and a safety-interlocked nitrous controller — are running on a real 2014 Stingray. Signals validated against the C7 community VBOX reference and the opendbc Cadillac CT6 powertrain DBC. Passive CAN read; no reflash.
